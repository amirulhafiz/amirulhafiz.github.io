Biography

Dr. Amirul Hafiz is a dedicated and hardworking medical professional currently serving as a Houseman in a government hospital. With a strong passion for helping others, Dr. Hafiz has committed himself to providing high-quality patient care and making a positive impact in the lives of those he serves. He received his medical degree from UKM, one of the top medical schools in Malaysia, where he excelled academically and demonstrated a strong commitment to the field of medicine. His dedication to his work and commitment to lifelong learning has earned him the respect and admiration of his colleagues and patients alike. As he continues to grow in his career, Dr. Amirul Hafiz is poised to make a significant and lasting impact in the field of medicine.
